What We Do

Hospitals are always in need of materials, especially right now during the coronavirus outbreak. There are many people willing to help during these tough times, but typically they don't know what hospital needs what materials. This is where this website comes in; we allow hospitals to input their address and whetever items they need. This will show up on a map on the Donate tab of our site, which will connect the donators to the hospitals.

How to slow the spread of COVID-19

If you or anyone in your household has tested positive for the coronavirus, is an older person, or has a serious underlying health condition, everyone in your household should stay home and away from others. Absolutely do not go to school or work. Contact your medical provider. Wearing cloth face masks are recommended by the CDC, especially in public settings(eg. grocery stores and pharmacies).
